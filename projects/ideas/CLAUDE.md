### 2. Nasdaq Trading from Singapore

Practical research on setting up Nasdaq trading from Singapore with full tax optimization. Researched by Frankie (2026-04-01).

**Status**: Active research -- Sascha opening brokerage account soon.

#### Key Findings
- **Capital gains**: 0% tax (Singapore has no capital gains tax; US doesn't tax non-resident aliens)
- **Dividends**: 30% US withholding (reduced to 15% via Ireland-domiciled ETFs)
- **Growth stock strategy**: Focus on TSLA, NVDA, AMZN, META, GOOG (zero/low dividends = 100% tax-free returns)
- **Irish ETF hack**: Buy London-listed Irish-domiciled ETFs (CSPX, CNDX, VUSA) instead of US-listed (VOO, QQQ, VTI) for 15% dividend tax instead of 30%

#### Recommended Brokers
1. **Interactive Brokers (IBKR)** -- best overall (professional, $0 commission, direct market access)
2. **Webull Singapore** -- easiest + cheapest (mobile app, $0 commission)
3. **Moomoo Singapore** -- best for beginners
4. **Tiger Brokers** -- MAS regulated, popular in SG
5. **Longbridge** -- zero commission lifetime

#### Quick Start
- Trading hours: 9:30 PM - 4:00 AM SGT
- W-8BEN form required (declares non-US person status)
- Account setup: 10-15 minutes

Full details: `nasdaq-trading-singapore.md`
