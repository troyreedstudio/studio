# THE GEOFENCE — HOW WE GUARANTEE ACCURACY

This is the core of our trust and verification system. Here's exactly how it works:

**Step 1 — The Request**
A Seeker requests eyes on "Nobu New York." LMC instantly draws a tight geofence around Nobu's exact GPS coordinates — a 30-50 metre invisible circle.

**Step 2 — Scout Dispatch**
Only Scouts physically inside that geofence receive the ping. If you're standing at the restaurant next door — you don't get the ping. The system knows exactly where you are.

**Step 3 — Visual Confirmation**
Before a Scout starts filming, the app shows them a reference photo of the venue entrance. One tap: "Are you here?" If they're at the wrong spot — they know immediately.

**Step 4 — GPS-Stamped Submission**
When the Scout submits the clip, the app stamps it with their precise GPS coordinates at the exact moment of filming. If those coordinates don't match the geofence — the clip is rejected automatically. The Scout doesn't get paid.

**Step 5 — AI Signage Verification**
Our AI scans the submitted clip for visible signage. If a Scout submits a clip for Nobu but the sign says Soho House — the clip is flagged, rejected, and the Seeker gets a full refund.

**Step 6 — Cooldown Protection**
Once a Scout responds to a request at a location, they enter a 20-minute cooldown for that specific venue. No spam. No gaming the system.

**The result:** The most trusted, verified, real-time visual intelligence network ever built.
