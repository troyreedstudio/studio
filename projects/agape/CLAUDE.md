# Agape 26

Premium lifestyle brand anchored in high consciousness and unconditional love. 50/50 partnership between Sascha Gray and Troy Reed. Product lineup: caps, vest tops, premium hoodies. Strong margins (5-8x on caps/singlets).

**Tagline**: "Wear your intention."

**Status**: Brand development -- logo locked, content calendar written, avatar 26 live on HeyGen, Instagram @agape26collection launched, website agape26.com live, 23 ambassador images sourced.

## Brand Identity (LOCKED)

### Meaning
- **Agape**: Highest form of love -- unconditional
- **26**: Highest level of consciousness
- **Math**: 2 + 6 = 8 (infinity) -- love without end

### Tone
Intentional, elevated, minimal. No exclamation marks -- ever. Say less. Feel more. Periods for emphasis. Never explain the image -- add to it. Captions under 3 lines.

### Five Pillars
1. **Love** -- highest human energy, foundation of everything
2. **Balance** -- harmony of opposites (strength/softness, ambition/stillness)
3. **Creation** -- love expressed through design and building
4. **Legacy** -- what's built from love outlasts everything
5. **Silence** -- where clarity lives, where purpose is found

### The New Luxury
Health. Silence. Freedom to travel and return changed. Owning your time. Protecting your energy. Choosing how you move through the world.
