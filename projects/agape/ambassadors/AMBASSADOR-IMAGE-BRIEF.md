# Agape 26 — Global Ambassador Image Brief

## What We Need
One portrait photo per ambassador. These will become HeyGen AI avatars for 15-second social videos.

## Image Requirements
- **Head & shoulders shot**, front-facing, looking at camera
- **Clean/neutral background** (plain wall, studio, minimal clutter)
- **Well-lit** — natural or studio lighting, no heavy shadows
- **Clear face** — no sunglasses, no hands blocking face
- **Realistic** — real photos work best (AI-generated images can cause lip-sync issues)
- **Aesthetically pleasing & relatable** — beautiful but approachable, not stock-photo-perfect

## The Vibe
Each person should look like a **global citizen** — someone who stands out in a crowd. Think: distinctive, memorable, fashion-forward. The kind of person you'd stop and look at twice.

## Distinctive Features to Include
Mix these across the 39 ambassadors:
- Tattoos (neck, hand, sleeve, chest, collarbone)
- Piercings (nose ring, septum, lip, eyebrow, ear gauges, cartilage)
- Bold hair (colored, shaved sides, buzz cuts, undercuts, streaks, braids)
- Glasses (stylish frames, bold colors)
- Headwear (wraps, headbands, beanies)
- Unique clothing (leather, linen, streetwear, cultural elements, blazers)

## Where to Find Images
Free stock photo sites with diverse, high-quality portraits:
- **Unsplash** — unsplash.com (search: "[nationality] woman/man portrait")
- **Pexels** — pexels.com
- **Pixabay** — pixabay.com
- **Generated Photos** — generated.photos (AI faces, very realistic)
- **This Person Does Not Exist** — thispersondoesnotexist.com (random AI faces)
