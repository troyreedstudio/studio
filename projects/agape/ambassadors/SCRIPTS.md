# Ambassador Scripts — 15 Second Social Videos
*Natural, conversational, thought-provoking. Themes: reclaim your time, silence, real connection, cut the noise, ditch the scroll. 35-40 words each. Every script closes with "Agape 26."*

---

## ✅ Approved Scripts (Sascha's picks)

**1.** "I traded the noise for silence. The doom scroll for stillness, followers for friends. And honestly, I got my whole life back. Agape 26."

**2.** "They asked me what luxury means. I said waking up with nothing to prove to anyone. Agape 26."

**3.** "Everybody's connected but nobody's actually present. I chose to be here, fully, no filter. Agape 26."

**4.** "I sat in silence for the first time and I cried. Not because I was broken, but because I finally heard how strong I was. Agape 26."

**5.** "I used to chase validation like it was oxygen. Then I stopped and took a breath. Turns out I could breathe the whole time. Agape 26."

**6.** "The hardest conversation I ever had was with myself. No distractions, no music, no phone, just the truth. That was the beginning for me. Agape 26."
