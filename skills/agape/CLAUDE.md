# Agape 26 — Skill

## Brand

- **Name**: Agape 26 (patented)
- **Meaning**: Agape = highest form of love (unconditional). 26 = highest level of consciousness.
- **Positioning**: Premium lifestyle brand anchored in high consciousness — authentic to founders' actual lifestyle, not manufactured
- **Audience**: Unisex, women as primary consumers
- **Tier**: Premium basics with meaning (think Loewe/Prada singlet tier)
- **Tone**: Intentional, elevated, minimal. Say less. Feel more. No exclamation marks — ever.
- **Tagline**: "Wear your intention."
